Get your Magento store ready for GST

The advent of GST has affected all aspects of trade and commerce. Even the online marketplaces cannot escape the changes brought about by GST and are compelled to embrace this regime. Studies say that GST could be the key to unlocking most of the issues that are being faced by e-commerce sector when it comes to bearing indirect taxes. We act as a key-bearer by providing GST extension for Magento based e-commerce website. We provide GST extension that is compatible with the existing e-commerce website and smoothly assimilate with your existing platform.

Codilar GST Extension

Key features

  • Admin configuration for entering GST number specific to the store
  • Only the Invoice and Order templates that confirm with GST standards are updated
  • All the Invoice and order emails or pdf files are attached with GST number
  • Separate columns for showing SGST, CGST, and IGST
  • SGST, CGST, and IGST are automatically alculated and added to all transactional emails and pdf files
  • GST columns as per the standard format issued by Govt. of India for order email, in admin panel and in order pdf etc.
  • Optional buyer level GSTIN included in invoice.
